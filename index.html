ADJUSTABLE 3-D PRINTED MOBILE PHONE HOLDER
'PROTOTYPE'

The Three-Degree Mobility Mobile Phone Holder is designed to offer flexibility, stability, and ease of use, accommodating various phone sizes. The holder features three degrees of mobility, allowing users to adjust height, viewing angle, and phone grip. It consists of multiple replaceable parts, ensuring ease of manufacturing and maintenance. The design is optimized for 3D printing with minimal support structures, making it compatible with small-sized printers.

The holder includes a sturdy base for stability, adjustable vertical and horizontal arms for height and angle modifications, and moving and fixed jaws to fit different phone sizes. Ball joints and other mechanisms ensure smooth adjustments, enhancing user comfort.

Assembly is straightforward, with easily replaceable parts that do not require specialized tools, making maintenance simple. The design can be printed using materials like PLA or ABS with a recommended layer height of 0.2 mm and 40% infill, ensuring durability.
